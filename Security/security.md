# Venue Security Rider

## A. SECURITY GUIDELINE OVERVIEW
1. Security for the Linkin Park 2025 Tour is a collaborative effort. This document confirms the best practices implemented for venue security and crowd management.
2. A comprehensive security staffing plan has been submitted, including: Security personnel assignments, Total staffing numbers, A dot map indicating security positions, A cost estimate.
3. Communication between the tour team, local security leads, and public safety officials is established.
4. An interpreter is available on site throughout the day.
5. Only authorized uniformed security/staff may access: Backstage, On-stage areas, Barricade areas.
6. Local security DOs: Wear easily identifiable uniforms, have a working flashlight. DO NOTs: Possess firearms, tasers, pepper spray or any weapon.
7. Tour security reserves the right to request the removal of any guard.

## B. SHOW STOP PROCEDURES
1. In an emergency (e.g., bomb threat, civil unrest), the artist security team will be notified immediately.
2. The venue provides: A written emergency and evacuation action plan, A public safety contact list, Clear map of EMS locations, dressing rooms, egress routes and assembly areas.

## C. REPORTING
1. An incident summary report will be provided post-event covering: Medical incidents, Police contacts, Refusals of entry, Ejections, Any significant security concerns.

## D. VENUE ACCESS & SCREENING
1. The tour does not require a backstage police presence. Backstage and barricade are clear of police unless requested.
2. Alternative security measures have been outlined and confirmed.
3. Entry protocols include handheld metal detector wands and bag searches.
4. **Prohibited Items:** Professional cameras/video/audio, selfie sticks, iPads, drones, fireworks, lasers, food, cans, bottles, alcohol, weapons, spiked bracelets, backpacks, waist packs, projectiles.
5. Letters/gifts from patrons will be inspected by tour security.
6. Prohibited items signage is displayed at all entry points.
7. An adequate number of female security staff is provided to search female patrons.

## E. PASSES
1. Access to tour-related areas is strictly limited to personnel with tour-issued credentials.
2. All credentials must be worn and visible at all times.

## F. COMMUNICATION
1. A WhatsApp group is created for day-of-show communications. Contacts for key venue staff have been provided.

## G. CROWD DYNAMICS
1. Crowd surfing and moshing may occur. Patrons will only be removed if their behavior is a safety concern.
2. Barricade security will be respectful and guest-friendly.

## H. SECURITY POSTS & RESPONSIBILITIES
1. A detailed staffing schedule is provided covering: Dressing rooms, Stage & backstage, Parking zones.
2. Truck & bus areas remain secured. Offsite parking is manned.
3. Designated parking is provided for 10 artist/band vehicles and management vehicles.

## I-J. SOUND CHECK & BARRICADE
1. Sound checks are closed to unauthorized personnel.
2. Barricade for GA floor: Configured to reduce rear pressure. FOH positions fully secured.

## K-M. BOMB SWEEP, ESCORT & CCTV
1. A bomb detection sweep is confirmed for 2 PM on show day.
2. A police escort is confirmed for artist arrival/departure.
3. No recording permitted near dressing rooms/backstage without approval.

## N-P. CONCESSIONS, MEETINGS & MEDICAL
1. Concessions: Beverages in soft crushable cups. Crushed ice only. No vending near stage.
2. Security meeting is scheduled with promoter rep, security director, medical lead, and supervisors.
3. EMS/First Aid: Locations of personnel, AEDs, and oxygen are mapped. Evacuation policy is confirmed and crew hydration coolers are maintained.
