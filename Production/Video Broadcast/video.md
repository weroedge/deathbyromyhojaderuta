For this show, **2 side screens** for live broadcast and **1 central screen (12x6m)** will be installed.

The signal for the central screen can be received at FOH or on stage via HDMI.

Additionally, the live broadcast production will feature a **3-camera setup**:

- 1 camera at FOH.
- 2 cameras in the pit in front of the stage.
